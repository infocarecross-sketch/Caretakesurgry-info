What We Offer

All Services at CareCross Surgery

Our practice is equipped to handle a broad range of primary healthcare needs. Below is a full overview of all 22 services we provide.

🩺

GP Consultations & Care Plans

Thorough general practitioner consultations for acute and ongoing conditions. Dr Prefa takes time to listen, examine and develop a clear clinical care plan for every patient — adults and children welcome.

Chronic Disease Management

Ongoing monitoring and clinical management of hypertension, diabetes mellitus (Type 1 & 2), asthma, HIV, tuberculosis and other chronic conditions. Regular follow-up visits ensure your condition remains well controlled.

HypertensionDiabetesAsthmaFollow-Up Care

HIV Counselling & Testing

Consultation Fees & Payment

We keep our consultation fees accessible. Both medical aid and private payment options are accepted.

💳 Medical Aid Patients

We welcome patients from most major medical aid schemes. Please bring your medical aid card and a valid form of identification.

💵 Private Patients

Private consultation fees are kept competitive. Please contact us for current consultation rates.

📋 SASSA Patients

SASSA assessment fees follow standard medical guidelines. Please bring your ID and any prior medical records.
