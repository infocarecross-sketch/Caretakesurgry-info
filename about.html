Meet Your Doctor

Our Doctor

Our Doctor is a qualified and HPCSA-registered general practitioner with over 17 years of clinical experience spanning healthcare settings across Nigeria, Lesotho and South Africa. As the founder and principal practitioner of CareCross Surgery, Dr Prefa brings deep clinical knowledge and a genuine commitment to the Piet Retief community.

CareCross Surgery is built on the principle that every patient deserves professional, compassionate and dignified clinical care. From chronic disease management and family medicine to SASSA assessments and after-hours virtual consultations — we are here for every stage of your healthcare journey.

Clinical Excellence
