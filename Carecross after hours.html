How It Works

Getting Your After-Hours Consultation

Booking an after-hours virtual consultation is quick and simple. Follow these three steps:

1

Contact Us

Call or WhatsApp us on 071 046 5187 between 17:00 and 22:00 to request your virtual consultation.

2

Confirm Details

Provide your name, medical aid details and a brief description of your health concern. We will confirm your slot.

3

Consult Virtually

Our practitioner will contact you at the agreed time via phone or video call to conduct your consultation.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Who qualifies for after-hours virtual consultations?

After-hours virtual consultations are currently available for medical aid patients. Please have your medical aid details ready when you contact us.

What conditions can be assessed virtually?

Virtual consultations are suitable for many non-emergency conditions including flu symptoms, minor infections, follow-up care, chronic disease queries, and general health concerns. For emergencies please contact your nearest emergency service.

How do I pay for the virtual consultation?

The consultation will be billed directly to your medical aid scheme. Please have your medical aid membership number available at the time of booking.

Can I get a sick note after a virtual consultation?

Yes. If clinically indicated, a medical certificate or sick note can be issued following your virtual consultation and sent to you electronically.

What if my condition requires an in-person visit?

If our practitioner determines that your condition requires an in-person examination, you will be advised accordingly and an appointment will be arranged for the next available consulting day.

What are the after-hours virtual consultation times?

After-hours virtual consultations are available daily from 17:00 to 22:00, including weekends and public holidays.

Need a Doctor After Hours?

Call or WhatsApp us between 17:00 and 22:00 daily. We are here when you need us most.
